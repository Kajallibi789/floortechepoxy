EPU Floorings

EPU Flooring Service
EPU floorings offer unmatched versatility and durability, making them ideal for a wide range of applications. Engineered with advanced technology, these floors provide exceptional resistance to abrasion, chemicals, and heavy foot traffic. Whether in industrial settings, commercial spaces, or residential areas, EPU floorings deliver reliable performance and long-lasting aesthetics, ensuring a seamless and functional environment
The new generation EPU based floor toppings offer excellent abrasion resistance, flexibility, chemical resistance and fast curing properties. EPU is a multi polymer, hybrid of epoxy and PU, where the functional properties of both the products are combined to impart the optimum properties. It scores over conventional epoxies as well as polyurethanes in terms of performance. Its end use applications include automobile plants, pharma, food processing industries, chemical plants etc.
Features :

  • Excellent Adhesion

  • Insensitive To Moisture

  • Highly Flexible

  • Excellent Abrasion And Impact Resistance

  • Excellent Gloss And Self Levelling Properties

  • Good Chemical Resistance. No Cracks On Joints

  • Flexo Hard And Fast Setting

  • No Bubbles
